About the Author



My name is David Bradford and I am in my third semester of the Government Analytics master's program at Johns Hopkins University. I live in Berkeley, CA. Before starting the program, I worked for two years at a political consulting firm in San Francisco, implementing strategy for candidate and ballot measure campaigns. I am currently a Political Analyst Fellow at Crowdpac, a website that uses data to provide objective information on federal, state, and local candidates, such as where they stand on issues and who has donated to their campaign.

At the conclusion of my master's program, I plan on going into political polling or a more general data science field. I have a working knowledge of R, Stata, and SQL, and hope to become proficient in Python as well.
