Methodology

These are the methodological steps I will take to answer my research question.

  • Figure out the percentage of the popular vote that both Clinton and Sanders received in each county using 2016 Presidential Primary election results data from Kaggle.
  • Use U.S. Census data to determine the racial/ethnic demographics of each county in the U.S. I am considering determining a coding system for each county that indicates whether the county has a "very high," "somewhat high," "medium," "somewhat low," or "very low" black population. I will need to figure out whether a coding system like that will be useful.
  • Determine what region of the country each county is in (i.e. South, Northeast, Midwest, etc.).
  • Use the National Center for Health Statistics' Urban-Rural Classification Scheme data to determine the urban/rural level of each county.
  • From these datasets, I can compare Sanders' share of the black vote in different geographic regions of the country, as well has his share in urban vs. rural counties. I'll use CNN exit poll data to corroborate and add context to county-level data.
